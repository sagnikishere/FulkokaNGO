Our Programs

Education & Community Library

Bridging the knowledge gap, one book at a time.

Education is the foundation of empowerment. In many parts of Bihar, access to quality learning resources remains a challenge. Fulkoka is launching a Community Library & Resource Centre (CLRC) to provide a safe, inclusive space where knowledge is accessible to all.

  • Access to books, newspapers, and digital resources
  • Quiet study spaces & Career guidance
  • Digital literacy training for youth

Addressing the root causes of health disparity.

Environment & Climate Change

Building resilience for a greener future.

The health of people and the planet are deeply interconnected. Rising temperatures and unpredictable weather patterns in Bihar affect agriculture and spread vector-borne diseases. We empower communities to become active stewards of their environment.

  • Promoting sustainable agriculture
  • Restoration of local water bodies (Ponds/Pokhars)
  • Climate-resilient health systems
