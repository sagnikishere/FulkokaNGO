About Fulkoka

Fulkoka is a not-for-profit organization registered under Section 8 of the Companies Act, Government of India. Our name is inspired by the Maithili word for the wild water lily.

We focus on creating systemic change that improves health outcomes and promotes sustainable well-being for all.
